*Romeo and Juliet* is a tragic love story by William Shakespeare, set in Verona. It tells the tale of two young lovers, Romeo and Juliet, who come from feuding families, the Montagues and Capulets. Despite their families' hatred, they fall in love and secretly marry. However, a series of misunderstandings and conflicts lead to a tragic ending where both die, hoping to be together in peace. The play explores themes of love, fate, and the consequences of family feuds.
